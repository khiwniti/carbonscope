cBIM AI Logo Concepts

Three original directions for sustainable construction intelligence

1

Carbon Cycle

A continuous circular flow representing lifecycle carbon assessment (EN 15978). The flowing loop symbolizes cradle-to-grave tracking with an integrated arrow showing forward progress and sustainability commitment.

Design Rationale

Circular economy principles meet data flow visualization. The gap creates visual tension while maintaining smooth continuity.

2

Data Lattice

An isometric cube wireframe representing BIM spatial intelligence and structured data architecture. The 3D perspective conveys depth, precision, and the multi-dimensional nature of carbon assessment.

Design Rationale

Technical precision meets architectural thinking. The wireframe suggests transparency and data-driven methodology.

3

Growth Nexus

An organic leaf form constructed from geometric precision lines. Balances natural sustainability (organic shape) with technical rigor (geometric construction), representing the intersection of nature and data.

Design Rationale

Biomimicry meets digital precision. The dual nature reflects sustainable construction through intelligent systems.
