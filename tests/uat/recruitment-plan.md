# UAT Participant Recruitment Plan

## Target: 10+ Thai Construction Professionals

## Participant Profiles

### Profile 1: Architects (3 participants)
**Requirements:**
- Licensed architect in Thailand (ASA member preferred)
- 2+ years experience in sustainable building design
- Familiar with TREES/EDGE certification (bonus)
- Has worked with BOQ files in projects
- Fluent in Thai, comfortable with technical terminology

**Why this profile:**
- Primary users of carbon assessment tools
- Decision-makers in material selection
- Need to communicate sustainability to clients

### Profile 2: Construction Engineers (3 participants)
**Requirements:**
- Civil/structural engineer with construction site experience
- 2+ years managing BOQ and material procurement
- Familiar with Thai construction materials and suppliers
- Experience with Excel-based project management
- Fluent in Thai, technical English (bonus)

**Why this profile:**
- Hands-on experience with materials and quantities
- Practical knowledge of cost implications
- Key stakeholders in material substitution decisions

### Profile 3: Sustainability Consultants (2 participants)
**Requirements:**
- TREES AP (Accredited Professional) or EDGE Auditor certification
- 2+ years in green building consulting
- Experience preparing TREES/EDGE documentation
- Knowledge of embodied carbon calculations
- Bilingual (Thai/English) preferred

**Why this profile:**
- Expert users who can validate technical accuracy
- Can assess credibility of certification features
- Provide high-quality feedback on methodology

### Profile 4: Project Managers (2 participants)
**Requirements:**
- 3+ years managing construction projects in Thailand
- Responsible for budget and sustainability targets
- Works with multiple stakeholders (architects, engineers, clients)
- Experience reviewing consultant reports
- Fluent in Thai, business English (bonus)

**Why this profile:**
- End decision-makers who balance cost and sustainability
- Need clear, actionable insights from tools
- Can assess business value proposition

## Selection Criteria

### Must-have criteria:
- ✅ 2+ years relevant experience (or graduate student in relevant field)
- ✅ Fluent in Thai
- ✅ Has worked with BOQ files or equivalent
- ✅ Available for 1-hour session
- ✅ Consents to recording

### Preferred criteria (bonus points):
- 🌟 TREES AP or EDGE Auditor certification
- 🌟 Experience with carbon calculations or LCA
- 🌟 Multiple project types (residential, commercial, industrial)
- 🌟 Bilingual (Thai/English)

### Diversity targets:
- Mix of company sizes (large firms, SMEs, freelancers)
- Geographic diversity (Bangkok, regional cities)
- Gender diversity (aim for 40-60% split)
- Experience levels (junior, mid-level, senior)

## Logistics

### Session Format
- **Platform:** Zoom or Google Meet
- **Duration:** 60 minutes (90 minutes for experts)
- **Recording:** With participant consent
- **Materials:** Sent 1 day before session (test credentials, BOQ sample files)

### Session Structure
- 0-5 min: Introduction and consent confirmation
- 5-50 min: Task execution (6 scenarios)
- 50-60 min: Survey and open feedback
- 60+ min: Thank you and compensation processing

### Facilitator Training
- Review scenarios thoroughly
- Practice neutral observation (no leading)
- Prepare troubleshooting guide for technical issues
- Have backup facilitator available

### Data Management
- Anonymous participant IDs (P001, P002, etc.)
- Secure storage for recordings (encrypted, access-controlled)
- Separate personally identifiable information (PII) from research data
- GDPR/PDPA compliance (Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act)

## Success Metrics

### Recruitment success:
- ✅ 10+ qualified participants recruited within 3 weeks
- ✅ 40-60% diversity across profiles (architects, engineers, consultants, PMs)
- ✅ At least 2 TREES AP or EDGE Auditor certified participants
- ✅ <20% no-show rate

### Session quality:
- ✅ 90%+ participants complete all 6 scenarios
- ✅ Average session duration: 50-70 minutes
- ✅ 80%+ participants consent to follow-up contact

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low response rate | Use multiple channels, increase compensation, extend timeline |
| Unqualified applicants | Rigorous screening questionnaire, verify credentials |
| High no-show rate | Send reminders 24hr before, confirm on session day |
| Technical issues during session | Test platform beforehand, have backup facilitator, reschedule if needed |
| Language barriers | Provide bilingual materials, use Thai facilitator |
| Participant bias (too positive) | Emphasize that we want honest, critical feedback |
