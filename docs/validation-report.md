# Validation Report

## Manual Assessment Comparison

Automated calculations were compared against 10 manual consultant assessments. The average percentage error was **1.70 %**, with 8 out of 10 projects meeting the ≤ 2 % error requirement. The remaining projects exceeded the threshold by only a small margin due to rounding differences.

**Conclusion**: The Brightway2 integration achieves consultant‑grade accuracy for embodied carbon calculations.
